Terminology

The terminology for survey phases, activities and personnel can vary between organisations. The key terms used in this book are defined below.

Concept Definition
Data monitors The role conducting field checks.
Final field test At the end of fieldworker training for trainees to test all survey instruments and protocols and to assess team’s readiness for the field.
Interview The act of interviewing a respondent or a completed questionnaire with responses recorded.
Pilot The testing exercise conducted prior to fieldwork training to establish full functionality of survey instruments and protocols.
Questionnaire The form comprised group of questions.
